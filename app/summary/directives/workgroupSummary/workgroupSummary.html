Monday, August 30, 2021

New

  • [Helper Center] The IPA Help Center has been updated to aid in the onboarding of new users. Visit it at https://ipa.ucdavis.edu/help.html

  • [Instructors Module] Non-course related assignment options are now available.

  • [Workload Summary Report] New columns have been added to the Workload Summary Report: Year, Instructor Type, Instructor Name, Course Type.

  • [Schedule Summary Report] Instructor Types can now be toggled on and off.

  • [Activity Log] Activity logs can now be downloaded for individual modules.

  • Numerous additional improvements--too many to count--have been made by the IPA team, including onboarding new departments/schools.

  • Finally, IPA has been migrated to utilize cloud hosting, enabling additional improvements to the system.

Fixed

  • The IPA team has additionally implemented many security improvements, bug fixes, and quality of life enhancements across every module within IPA. Thanks to everyone who has reached out to us for support. Your success is our success!
