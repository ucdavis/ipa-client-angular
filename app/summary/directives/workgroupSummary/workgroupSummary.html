What's New

Thursday, March 4, 2021

New

  • [Courses Module]The term labels at the top of the Courses table are now always visible as users scroll.

  • [Registrar Reconciliation Report] Sections that are in Banner but not in IPA are now displayed, with options to filter by activity type.

  • [Workload Summary Report] Instructor notes (Assignments > By Instructor) are now included in the report.

Fixed

  • Additional security improvements, bug fixes, and quality of life enhancements.

Tuesday, January 5, 2021

New

  • Each department now has an Activity Log that can be access from the toolbar in top right of each module. Actions performed by users within each department are now logged and visible to departmental users in order to facilitate understanding and provide points of contact regarding work performed within IPA.

Fixed

  • Additional security improvements, bug fixes, and quality of life enhancements.
