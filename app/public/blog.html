IPA 1.0 Released

September 9th, 2016

On Friday, September 9th, 2016 the DSS IT Development Team released the first public version of IPA, designated 1.0 "SSH" (named for the complex building where the project began).

Development of the Teaching Assistant, Budget, and Banner Change Report components are on-going and will be available in future releases.

For now, the team is focusing on bug fixes, performance improvements, and providing help and support to new customers.
